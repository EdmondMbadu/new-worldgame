Join us for a transformative "Lunch and Learn" session, *Architects of the Future: The Essential AI Toolkit for Advancing the UN SDGs*, on December 12, 2024 at 12pm EST.

This workshop, sponsored by the NewWorld Game, is both a call to action and a celebration of innovation. Inspired by Buckminster Fuller’s vision that we are "architects of the future, not its victims," Participants will explore the power of artificial intelligence to accelerate solutions to global challenges defined by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). This session is designed for individuals looking to leverage AI to make meaningful, data-driven impacts on sustainable development initiatives.

Agenda Highlights:

  • The AI Advantage for SDGs: An exploration of AI’s unique abilities, from pattern recognition to predictive insights, that enhance efforts in poverty reduction, environmental preservation, and education equality.
  • Understanding AI’s Boundaries and Ethical Use: Discussion on AI’s limitations and the importance of responsible, ethical AI use to mitigate risks and ensure positive outcomes.
  • Hands-On with AI Tools for SDG Impact : Practical demonstration and mini-workshop using AI tools tailored for sustainability, focusing on real-world applications aligned with SDGs.
  • Q&A and Discussion: Interactive session for attendees to discuss challenges, ideas, and potential collaborations.
