Upcoming NWG Features.

The upcoming NWG features will include:

  • Multiple AI Colleagues: Soon you will be able to add Nelson (Nelson Mandela), Al (Albert Einstein), Rachel (Rachel Carson), and many more as NWG team colleagues.
  • Start a Tournament: Soon you will be able to start a tournament and fund solutions based on problems that interest you.
  • Invite people around the world: Soon you will be able to invite people from around the world to join your team to solve problems that interest you.
