NewWorld Game Evaluator

A critical part of NewWorld Game is the Evaluator. As an Evaluator you become part of the Design Team that develops the solution. Your evaluation and comments are essential for improving the solution so it will be implemented in the real world. The following is a guide for what you are to do: Your role as an Evaluator is to look over the strategy that has been submitted and, as best you can, come up with ways for improving it, making it stronger, and more likely to get implemented in the real world.

Numerical Evaluations— Sliders

NewWorld Game has a set of measures that are used to evaluate a solution. These include the following:

  • Does the solution achieve the Preferred State
  • Is it technologically feasible — does the solution use current technology (no magic wands)
  • Is it ecologically positive — does it do no harm, preserve, or better, regenerate the environment (destroying the environment is no solution)
  • Economical — is it affordable, is there a reasonable source for funding the strategy (no Santa Claus)
  • Equitable — is it fair, just, builds social cohesion, reduces wealth disparities (no subsidies to billionaires)
  • Understandable — is it straight-forward, organized so it can be understood by a high school student (and if you are a HS student—by your parents)

Each of the above has a sliding scale of 1 to 10. Move the slider to the number you think is an accurate representation of where the solution is relative to the evaluation criteria. (1 = no, not viable; 5 = could be, but needs work: 10 = yes, let’s do it, now)

This quantitative scoring system is very important. But more important is your qualitative feedback and suggestions for improvements to the solution. You add these in the “Comment” windows. If you put in a low score for a solution on any criteria there is a good reason for it. Put that/those reason(s) in the Comments. The designers who developed the solution will use your comments to improve their solution—and re-submit it to be evaluated again. When it reaches a certain numerical level, it is eligible to be submitted to the NewWorld Tournament.)
