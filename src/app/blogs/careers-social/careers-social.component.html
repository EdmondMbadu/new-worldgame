About NewWorld Game

NewWorld Game (NWG) is an AI-assisted, team-based platform where students and communities design evidence-based, sustainable, and equitable solutions to local & global challenges—aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

We’re seeking creative, mission-driven undergrads who love crafting posts, testing what works, and seeing ideas move people to action— especially if you care about climate, hunger, health, education, and more.

What you’ll do

  • 🧵 Draft and publish posts for NWG challenges across LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, X (Twitter), and YouTube.
  • ✍️ Translate challenge briefs into short, compelling copy, carousels, and short-form video scripts.
  • 📅 Plan a weekly content calendar; repurpose one idea across multiple formats and platforms.
  • 🤖 Help us leverage and build our own AI Content Platform.
  • 💬 Engage the community: reply to comments/DMs, highlight teams, and surface great user stories.
  • 🎨 Collaborate on visuals (Canva/CapCut welcome) and on-brand messaging.
  • 📈 Track simple metrics (reach, clicks, sign-ups) and propose quick A/B tests to improve.
  • 🌍 Spot trends and opportunities to join timely conversations in education, AI, climate, health, etc.
  • 🧭 Uphold voice & values: inclusive, optimistic, and grounded in evidence and impact.

You’ll thrive here if you…

  • 🧑‍🎓Are currently enrolled as an undergrad and write clean, punchy copy in English.
  • ⚡Live on social and understand what stops the scroll (hooks, pacing, visuals, CTAs).
  • 🧩Care about real-world problem solving (education, climate, health, equity, cities, etc.).
  • 🔁Are organized, curious, and iterate fast based on feedback and simple analytics.
  • 🏅Bonus: familiarity with Canva/CapCut, basic video editing, or running IG/TikTok/LinkedIn pages.

What you’ll gain

  • 🚀A ship-ready portfolio of live posts, campaigns, and growth snapshots with your byline.
  • 🤝Mentorship and feedback from a small team at the AI × education × social-impact edge.
  • 🌐Leadership experience mobilizing students and partners around SDG-aligned challenges.
  • 📣A strong reference / LinkedIn recommendation, and potential pathways to future paid roles.
  • 🎓Academic credit where available (we’ll support your paperwork with your school).
