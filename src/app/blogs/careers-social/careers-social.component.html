About NewWorld Game

NewWorld Game (NWG) is an AI-assisted, team-based platform where students and communities design evidence-based, sustainable, and equitable solutions to local & global challenges—aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We’re looking for creative, mission-driven undergraduate students to help us spark conversations and mobilize participation across our social channels.

If you love crafting posts, testing what works, blazing new channels, and seeing your ideas move people to action—this is for you. If you care about climate, hunger, health, education and other SDG-aligned challenges—this is for you.

What you’ll do

  • 🧵Draft and publish posts for NWG challenges across LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, X (Twitter), and YouTube.
  • ✍️Translate challenge briefs into short, compelling copy, carousels, and short-form video scripts.
  • 📅Plan a weekly content calendar; repurpose one idea across multiple formats and platforms.
  • 🤖Help us leverage and build our own AI Content Platform.
  • 💬Engage the community: reply to comments/DMs, highlight teams, and surface great user stories.
  • 🎨Collaborate on visuals (Canva/CapCut welcome) and on-brand messaging.
  • 📈Track simple metrics (reach, clicks, sign-ups) and propose quick A/B tests.
  • 🌍Spot trends and timely conversations in education, AI, climate, health, etc.
  • 🧭Uphold voice & values: inclusive, optimistic, and grounded in evidence and impact.

You’ll thrive here if you…

  • 🧑‍🎓Are currently enrolled as an undergrad and write clean, punchy copy in English.
  • ⚡Live on social and understand hooks, pacing, visuals, and CTAs.
  • 🧩Care about real-world problem solving (education, climate, health, equity, cities, etc.).
  • 🔁Are organized, curious, and iterate fast based on feedback and simple analytics.
  • 🏅Bonus: Canva/CapCut, basic video editing, or running IG/TikTok/LinkedIn pages.

What you’ll gain

  • 🚀A ship-ready portfolio of live posts, campaigns, and growth snapshots with your byline.
  • 🤝Mentorship and feedback from a small, energetic team at the AI × education × social-impact edge.
  • 🌐Leadership experience mobilizing students and partners around SDG-aligned challenges.
  • 📣A strong reference / LinkedIn recommendation; potential paths to future paid roles.
  • 🎓Academic credit where available (we’ll support your school paperwork).
