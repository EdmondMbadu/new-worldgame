How To Use NewWorld Game

This is a cheatsheet of everything in the NewWorld Game web application. If there is something that you wish to do but is not included here, please follow this link and add your suggestion.

1. Create an Account

To Create an account click on: Get Started at the very start of the landing page and follow the steps (enter your first name, last name, email, and password (twice), etc). At the end, an email will then be sent to you to verify your account.

2. Sign In

Once you have verified your email, you can now Sign In to your account (Check your spam in case you do not see the verification email).

To Sign In go to: Sign In and enter your email, and password. Voila, that is all you need to start developing solutions.

3. Home Page

Once you have signed in to your account, you will see the Home Page. The home page is composed of two sections - the navigation bar on the left, and the ready-made challenges in the middle.
- The navigaiton bar on the left section has a menu bar that contains a list of everything you need to navigate the NewWorld Game.
- The ready-made challenges in the middle, are challenges that you can use to jump right into solving problems without setting up your own solution.

3. My Workspace

The Workspace is where you’ll find all your solutions — whether they are pending, or already submitted. It serves as a personalized hub to track everything you’ve created or contributed to.

  • Search by title — easily filter through your solutions using a quick keyword search.
  • Click to open — selecting any solution takes you directly to its Solution Dashboard.

For more details about what you can do in a Solution Dashboard, scroll down to the Dashboard section.

4. Start a Solution

To start a solution, go the right of your home screen, and click on "Start a Custom Challenge". You can also start ready-made solution by just selecting one of the solution in the middle. This will lead you to a page with 6 fields to fill.
1. The first field is the general problem area your solution will be in
2. The second field is a working title for your solution.
3. The third field is a feature image for your solution.
4. The fourth field is a description of your solution. Use Chat with Bucky if you need help describing your solution in few bullet points. For help on how to ask question to Chat with Bucky see here.
5. The fifth field is "Add Team Members". You can add emails of all the members you want to invite. The members will then receive an email asking them to join your solution. If an invited member does not have an account, they should create one to join you.
6. This is not a field per say, but are randomly generated evaluators that will be evaluating your solution once it is submiited you can add more as well.
7. The last field is for Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) that correspond to your solution.
Once all fields completed, click "Launch Solution Lab" to start developing your solution.

5. Develop a Solution

Once you launch the solution, you are now ready to develop a solution. There are 5 steps - 4 development steps and one final review step. While developing your solution at each step, you can consult Chat With Bucky as a resource, or hover and click on the question mark next to each question to access 3 extra sources: ( Data Commons, Atlas SDG, SDG Tracker )

5.1 Define the Problem State

This is where you define the problem you are trying to solve. Each question allows you to dig deeper into the nature of the problem. To save your progress, scroll down and you will see the save button.
To go to the next step (step 2), scroll down and click next.

5.2 Envision the Preferred State

Now that you are done with, or have decided to move on from the first step, you are now on step 2: "Envision the Preferred State". This is where you envision where you want to get once your solution has been achieved/done. To track which step you are on, you can see where the red bar timeline points to. You can also move back and forth of each step by clicking at the appropriate step on the timeline.

5.3 Develop Our Plan

This is the third step on the list. This is where you develop a plan that will get you from the Problem State to the Preferred State. It contains 5 questions.

5.4 Implementation

This step is all about implementation. The resources- financial, materials, humans, needed to implement the plan.

5.5 Strategy Review

This is the final step where all the work comes together into a coherent whole

6. Save Solution

At each step, you can save your progess by simply scrolling to the bottom of the page and clicking save. The saved work will be under Pending Solutions (highlighted in red below) on the Home Page. You can then exit this page knowing that your progress has been saved.

7. Submit Solution

To Submit a solution, scroll to the bottom of the page ( Strategy Review Page) and submit your solution.

8. View Solution

To view your submitted solution, visit your Profile Page.

Below is the Profile Page. To get there, click under your name at the top right, and then click on Profile. This will lead to your profile page where you can add different credentials, change your profile picture, and also where all solutions that have been developed by you or where you were a member are located. To find your solution, you can also search for your solution on the search bar by title.

9. Edit Submitted Solution

To Edit a Submitted Solution, first you need to find that solution (See section 8 for that). Once you have located your solution, click on more to see the full view of the solution. Once on the full view of the solution, you will see an Edit Button right below the title. click on Edit to edit your solution. You will then find that solution under My Workspace on the navigation bar.

10. Delete Submitted Solution

There are cases where you want to delete your solution completely. The steps are similar to editing a solution. The only difference is that at the final stage, instead of Editing, click on Delete Solution.

11. Delete Pending Solution

You can also delete a pending solution. To do so, go to the My Workspace on the sidebar menu( see below), and click on the trash icon of the given solution to delete the pending solution.

12. Evaluate Submitted Solution

If you are selected to evaluate a solution ( you will receive an email stating that you have been invited to evaluate a solution), you can find the solution to evaluate on the navigation menu bar under Evaluations. You need expand the navigation menu bar ( see highlighted section in red below).

Once you click on the particular solution to evaluate you will be led to a screen where you can evaluate the solution. See here on how to evaluate a solution properly.
Once done, the evaluation will be appended to the solution as part of a grade that will be used for further consideration such as the tournament. So your grade is a very critical part of the process

13. Join a Tournament

To join a tournament, you need to have at least two evaluations from a given solution and have an average of 9 from these evaluations. Assuming that these requirements are met, click on Join a Tournament on the sidebar menu under "Tournament" on your Home page ( see picture below) to Join a tournament.

Once on that page, simply scroll to the bottom under "Your Solutions" and select the solution you want to send to the tournament and click "Add Solution To Tournament". You will be contacted if you win the tournament or if something is needed from you. Solutions that have been sent already to the tournament will then show up at the top under "Tournaments Joined"

14. Add Image To Text Editor

To add an image to the text editor, you can simply drag and drop the desired image. You can also click on the insert image ( see screenshot below) and add the image you want from your computer that way as well.

15. Solution Dashboard

The Solution Dashboard is your team’s central hub for managing and enhancing your solution-building experience. From here, you can access every key component needed to collaborate, iterate, and present your work to the world. Here's what it includes:

  • 1. Develop Solution: The main structured workspace where you define your problem, envision your goals, build a plan, implement it, and review your strategy.
  • 2. Video Chat Room: A dedicated always-open video room to hop on calls with your team anytime.
  • 3. Team Discussion: A real-time group chat to exchange ideas, leave updates, and coordinate actions with your teammates.
  • 4. Solution Details: Edit your solution's title, update the README, invite friends, and assign evaluators — all in one place.
  • 5. Documents & Presentations: Upload supporting files and create shareable presentations to showcase your solution.
  • 6. Whiteboard Brainstorming: A shared collaborative canvas to ideate visually, sketch concepts, or map out complex systems together.

16. Team Video Call Room

This is a dedicated video room for your entire team. It’s always open and persistent — so you can hop in for a quick check-in or hold a full meeting anytime. You don’t need to send invites; anyone from the team can join the call whenever they’re ready.

17. Team Discussion (Chat)

This is your team’s discussion space. You can post updates, chat in real time, or leave messages for other team members. All team members can access this space from the left-side navigation under “My Workspace”.

18. Solution Details (Edit Solution Title, Invite Members & Evaluators)

To edit the title of your solution, first you need to go the dashboard of that particular solution. This can be found under the navigation bar on the left under "My Workspace". Once on My Workspace, select the solution you want to edit. This will lead to a page as the one below.

Then select Solution Details. This will lead to a page where you can edit the title of the solution and invite members to join your solution. Click on the pencil next to the title to edit the title. To invite members simply click on the (+) sign just below "Designers" and add the emails of the members you want to invite. Similarly, you can remove members by clicking on the (-) below "Designers". Note that adding or removing member can you only be done by the creator of the solution adding or removing members one at a time.

19. Documents and Presentations

This is where your team can upload and share files — including documents, PDFs, and presentations — that support your solution. You can access this feature under your solution dashboard via “Documents”.

20. How to Add a Presentation or Document

To upload a document, go to your Solution Dashboard → “Documents & Presentations” → click on “Add Doc”. You’ll be asked to enter:

  • Title — a short label for the document.
  • Description — a brief explanation to help identify the file.
  • Attach File — upload a document in PDF, DOCX, or PPT format.

Once submitted, your document will be accessible to all team members. They will be able to view or download it directly from the dashboard.

Add a Presentation

To create a presentation, stay under the “Documents & Presentations” section, and click on “Add Presentation”. For each slide you want to add, you will be asked for:

  • Title — the slide’s heading.
  • Description — short supporting text for the slide.
  • Image — optional visual (you can add only image, only text, or both).

You can add as many slides as you want by clicking “Add Slide” after each entry. Once all slides are added, click on “Save Presentation” to publish and share it.

*Note: Each slide must have at least a title or an image — you need one of these to proceed.

21. Whiteboard Brainstorming

The whiteboard is a shared creative space where you and your team can brainstorm visually in real time. You can draw, add sticky notes, create diagrams, or map ideas together. Find this under your solution dashboard under “Whiteboard Brainstorming”.
