Custom GPT for Design Science Storyboards

These pictures were made with a custom ChatGPT called Story Illustrator: link The initial prompt was very generic, just to demonstrate proof of concept “Hi - please make a story about a village in Africa that wants solar panels on their health clinic.” More detailed prompts would yield more detailed and specific images. To begin, the GPT asks a handful of questions about style, tone and length, and then generated the following text and images in about 5 minutes: The first image shows a vibrant African village where community members, excitedly discussing plans, hold blueprints for solar panel installations, under a speech bubble "Brighter Future!"

The second image captures the community beginning to install solar panels on their health clinic, with a speech bubble "Teamwork Sparks Change!" above the collaborative scene.

The third image shows the completed solar panel installation on the health clinic, with the community celebrating around it, under "Power to Heal!" speech bubble.

The fourth image depicts the inside of the health clinic, now brightly lit by solar power, with a speech bubble stating "A New Dawn of Care!" above the hopeful scene.

The fifth image features a group of villagers learning about solar energy from an expert, highlighted by the speech bubble "Learning Lights the Way!"

The final image shows the village celebrating their sustainable energy success under solar-powered lights, with "Together We Shine!" marking this joyous occasion.
