NewWorld Tournament

The NewWorld Tournament.is an exciting option for all players/designers in the NewWorld Game. It is a way your solution can move from “game” to the real world.

Here's how:

There are a series of Tournaments in the NewWorld Game. Many of them will be focused on different topics. Some will be general. Winners will receive monetary Awards that are used to take their solution towards implementation in the real world. For some solutions that could involve developing a prototype “proof of concept” for their solution, or a key part of the solution. For some other solutions it could involve further study into impacts and benefits. And for a few, the Award will be enough to begin implementation. The Award funds whatever is needed to make your solution more compelling, implementable, real.

The path to the Tournament is straight forward. First you develop your solution. Then your Team’s solution is evaluated. During the evaluation process a randomly chosen group of NewWorld Game players (and sometimes outside-the-game experts) goes over your solution and provides feedback on ways to improve and strengthen your solution. Their role is to be a part of your Team, to help you develop the most compelling strategy ever. Plus, they give your solution a numerical score of 1 to 10 in the following areas:

  • Technological feasibility — does your solution use current technology (no magic wands)
  • Ecologically positive - does it do no harm, preserve, or better, regenerate the environment (destroying the environment is no solution)
  • Economic — is it affordable, is there a reasonable source for funding the strategy (no Santa Claus)
  • Equitable - is it fair, just, builds social cohesion, reduces wealth disparities (no subsidies to billionaires, fossil fuels, etc.)
  • Achieves the Preferred State - does it achieve the Preferred State, using the technology, programs, policies, funding, etc. described.
  • Understandable — is it straight-forward, organized so it can be understood by a high school student (and if you are a HS student—by your parents)

After this feedback and score from the Evaluator Team you have the option of taking your strategy to the next level by incorporating what you learned in the Evaluation process that you feel strengthens your solution. Then you submit your improved solution to the Evaluation Team again. When your strategy has an average score of 9.0 from at least 3 of the Evaluators, it is eligible to be submitted to the NewWorld Tournament. It will also be added to the Global Solutions Database.

Funding NewWorld Tournaments:

The larger the monetary award the sooner real world implementation can happen. It is our intention to have as many Tournaments each year as possible. The number of Tournaments and the size of the monetary prize are dependent on funders. When NWG began we funded the Tournament from the $250 tuition payments received from participants in the Global Solutions Lab. We will continue doing this, but we want to do more.
