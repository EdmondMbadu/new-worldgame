Ask Bucky Prompts for Global Solution Implementation:

Funding Prompts

What foundations, government agencies, or other funding sources would fund (insert a summary of your solutions here) in (insert the country where you will test and first implement the solution here)?

Partner Prompts

What non-governmental organizations, national, regional, and local government agencies or groups in (insert the country where you will test and first implement the solution here) would be a good partner for a program that (insert a summary of your solutions here)?

Vision of Solution Prompts
