State of the World Reports

TThe link below takes you to our collection of over 400 state-of-the-world reports from UN agencies, international research organizations and researchers. The reports are intended to add depth and breadth of understanding to the problems on which players of NewWorld Game choose to work. They add perspective, insight, and data to the importance, context, impacts, trends, history and possible solutions to the problems our world is facing. The reports are grouped by topic—such as Food, Energy, Climate, Health, Education, etc. The latest reports will be found in folders marked with the latest year the reports have been issued. To access a folder, double click on it.
