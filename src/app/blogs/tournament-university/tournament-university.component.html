The NewWorld Game is a platform, an AI-empowered work- and play-space, for discovery and developing solutions to real-world problems—and getting those solutions implemented. Its goal is to have real impact—to change the world—by providing you and millions of other like-minded creative individuals with space and tools for developing solutions to the problems and issues you care about. The NewWorld Game is where students develop solutions to global and/or local real-world problems, enter them in a NewWorld Tournament where they compete for prizes, recognition and real-world impact. This is made possible by the creative combination of AI and human intelligence. Learn more.
