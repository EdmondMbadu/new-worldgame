The NewWorld Game is a platform, an AI-empowered work- and play-space, for discovery and developing solutions to real-world problems—and getting those solutions implemented. Its goal is to have real impact—to change the world—by providing you and millions of other like-minded creative individuals with space and tools for developing solutions to the problems and issues you care about. The NewWorld Game Changemakers Tournament is where students use the NewWorld Game platform to develop solutions to global and/or local real-world problems, enter them in the Tournament, where they compete with other students and schools for prizes, recognition and real-world impact. These formally outlandish goals and the process for achieving them are made possible by the creative combination of AI and human intelligence. Learn more.
