The NewWorld Game is a platform, an AI-empowered work- and learning-space, for discovery and developing solutions to real-world problems—and getting those solutions implemented.

Its goal is to have real impact—to change the world—by providing you and your students with space and tools for developing solutions to the problems and issues you care about.

The NewWorld Game is where students learn the subject matter you teach while developing solutions to global and/or local real-world problems and enter them in a NewWorld Tournament where they compete for recognition and real-world impact.

This is made possible by the creative combination of AI and human intelligence. Learn more.
