Design Science

Design Science is a method for developing life preserving and enhancing solutions to society's problems. This methodology is inspired by the work of Buckminster Fuller and Medard Gabel through their work on the World Game and Global Solutions Lab— on which the NewWorld Game is based.

SDGs you can solve

The UN’S Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) aim to transform our world. They are a call to action to end poverty and inequality, protect the planet, and ensure that all people enjoy health, justice and prosperity. The NewWorld Game enables you to develop ways of achieving them.”
