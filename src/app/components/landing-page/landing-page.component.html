NewWorld Game: Shape the Future

NewWorld Game is a challenge like you’ve never seen—powered by boldness, creativity, collaboration, and a sense of fun. It’s where your vision builds the future you want. You’re not the victim—you’re the challenger.

Boldness Creativity Collaboration Persistence Playfulness

It’s not a fantasy game—it's real. You don’t need luck to win. You need a clear idea of the world you want and the courage to make it real. NewWorld Game will show you how—and you’ll show the world what you can do.

Compete with the so-called “leaders” to solve the problems you care about—political, social, economic, environmental. The rewards? For you and for the world.

Design Science • Methodology

Design Science

Design Science is the methodology used by NewWorld Game for developing life-preserving and enhancing solutions to society's problems. This methodology is inspired by the work of Buckminster Fuller and others through the World Game and Global Solutions Lab—on which the NewWorld Game is based. For details see Tools For Changing the World—A Design Science Primer .
