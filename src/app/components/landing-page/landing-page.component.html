Design Science

Design Science is the methodology used by NewWorld Game for developing life preserving and enhancing solutions to society's problems. This methodology is inspired by the work of Buckminster Fuller and others through their work on the World Game and Global Solutions Lab— on which the NewWorld Game is based.
